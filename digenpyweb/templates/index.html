Router companies distribute their routers with a predefined wireless password.
Those passwords can be usually recreated using an algorithm, wich makes them unsecure

Digenpy is a pentesting tool to recover those wireless default passwords.
With digenpy you can easily crack the passwords for most Spanish routers, among others.

Airoscript-NG

Digenpy integrates perfectly with airoscript-ng, an aircrack-ng user interface.
This makes wireless pentesting, even easier, just remember to enable the plugin or launch airoscript with airoscript-ng -p digenpy
